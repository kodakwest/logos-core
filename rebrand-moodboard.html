Making Straight the Paths

Brand identity and naming exploration for a unified biblical study platform (formerly Bible AI Search)

Name Candidates

LogOS

"The Operating System for Truth"

Meaning & Linguistics

A portmanteau of Logos (Greek: The Word, divine reason, the creative order) and OS (Operating System). It emphasizes the platform as a foundational layer rather than just an application.

Biblical Resonance

"In the beginning was the Word (Logos)..." (John 1:1). Represents Christ as the creative force and ultimate truth. Just as an OS runs a computer, the Logos is the underlying logic of the universe.

Brand Feel

Highly modern, authoritative, and tech-forward, yet deeply rooted in foundational theology. It appeals to power-users, pastors, and deep-divers who want a robust "engine" for their study.

Ecosystem Naming

  • LogOS Core (The Main Hub)
  • LogOS Shepherd (Kids / Guided)
  • LogOS Roundtable (Sermon Collab)

Hodos

"Your Pathway to the Word"

Meaning & Linguistics

Greek (ὁδός) for way, path, road, or journey.

Biblical Resonance

Early Christians were known as followers of "The Way" (Hodos). Isaiah 40:3 commands to "make straight in the desert a highway (hodos in the Septuagint) for our God." Jesus says, "I am the way (hodos)..."

Brand Feel

Navigational, guiding, and active. It feels like an invitation to explore and walk through scripture rather than just searching it. Clean, memorable, and distinctive.

Kleidion

"Unlock the Scriptures"

Meaning & Linguistics

Greek (κλειδίον) meaning small key or simply key.

Biblical Resonance

Jesus speaks of the "key of knowledge" (Luke 11:52) and giving the "keys of the kingdom" (Matthew 16:19). It represents unlocking mysteries and gaining access to divine truth.

Brand Feel

Revelatory, secure, and empowering. It implies the AI is the key that opens up difficult or historical texts for the modern reader.

Euthys

"The Direct Path to Scripture"

Meaning & Linguistics

Greek (εὐθύς) meaning straight or immediately.

Biblical Resonance

Used heavily in the Gospel of Mark to denote immediate action, and famously by John the Baptist: "Make straight (euthys) the way of the Lord."

Brand Feel

Dynamic, efficient, and clarifying. It emphasizes the AI's ability to cut through noise and deliver direct, straight answers and pathways to biblical understanding.

Derech

"The Ancient Path, Anew"

Meaning & Linguistics

Hebrew (דֶּרֶךְ) for way, road, distance, journey, or manner.

Biblical Resonance

Proverbs 3:6: "In all your ways (derech) acknowledge Him, and He shall direct your paths." Psalm 119:105: "Your word is a lamp to my feet and a light to my path (derech)."

Brand Feel

Earthy, ancient, and deeply reverent. It connects the high-tech AI platform to the ancient, historical roots of the biblical text, bridging the gap between millennia.

Visual Direction

Dark, modern, contemplative. Like reading a sacred text by the warm glow of a lamp, augmented by the sharp clarity of modern technology.

Typography

Playfair Display

For Headings, Titles, and Sacred Text. Brings a classic, editorial, and historical weight to the brand. Echoes the typography of traditional printed Bibles.

Inter

For UI elements, body copy, and technical details. Highly legible, modern, and clean. Provides the "Tech/OS" balance to the classical headings.

Logo Concepts

The Open Path

A winding road that perfectly shapes the pages of an open Bible, drawing the eye upwards towards the horizon. The central dotted line represents both the spine of the book and the "straight path" ahead.

Symbolism

The Book: The foundation of scripture.
The Road: The journey of study and understanding.
The Horizon: Revelation and deeper connection with God.

Application

This logo works exceptionally well as a standalone app icon. It can scale down smoothly and fits perfectly within a circular or rounded-square app container.

The Networked Truth

AI nodes and connections forming a subtle cross or a star of Bethlehem, representing technology guiding the way.

The Key of Logos

An ancient key silhouette with a modern, geometric finish. The handle subtly incorporates an inner cross.

Brand Family Ecosystem

How the naming convention extends across the unified product suite.

[Brand] Core

The Central Hub

The primary entry point. Formerly 'Bible AI Search'. This is the central repository, search engine, and AI chat interface for deep theological study and exploration of the text.

[Brand] Shepherd

Kids Ministry

A guided, simplified, and visually engaging pathway for children. The 'Shepherd' branding fits perfectly within the 'Way/Path' concept, as a shepherd leads the flock along the right paths.

[Brand] Roundtable

Sermon Study

A collaborative, in-depth analysis tool for sermons and pastoral prep. Fits into the ecosystem as the 'gathering place' along the journey, where believers pause to dissect the Word together.
